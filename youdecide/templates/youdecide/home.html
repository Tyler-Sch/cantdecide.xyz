Welcome to a very early version of youdecide.xyz and thank you for helping me test it out



Currently, the website offers you a recipe from seriouseats.com, one of my personal favorites, and constructs a grocery list for cooking that meal.

If you like the sound of the recipe and would like another, click the green button. If you'd like something else, push the red.

That's pretty much it.

Thank you for testing my little project. If you have any thoughts on how to improve it, I'd love to know

Oh, and, the grocery list isnt always the most accurate thing. It's like 90% accurate, so if you see a recipe that you are excited about, just double check the ingredients from the actual recipe by clicking the link (the name of the recipe)
